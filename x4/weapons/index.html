Weapons

Last updated: 19/3/2021 (v4.0)

Below is a list of weapons in X4, and some quick notes about the columns below;
  • QTY = Quantity fired per shot (ie. one shot could spawn multiple projectiles, or have multiple barrels ie. like a double barrel shotgun),
  • RoF = Rate of fire / second.
  • S/S.DMG = The amount of shield damage done to a shielded target,
  • US/H.DMG = The amount of hull damage done to a unshielded target,
