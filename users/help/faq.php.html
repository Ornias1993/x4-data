I cant find my account?!?
All account names go through a filter which may change the username slightly. This is to filter out foreign letters (and spaces) from the name. You can check the account to find out how your username has been filtered, and see the current status of your account. Just visit the page and enter in your email address.


Ive been banned, why?
If you feel that your have been banned unfairly, please contact me. Bans are very rare unless you have broken the rules!
