I like this ship because it's one of the cheapest TLs out there. It's also the easiest to get. And it's the second fastest. I invariably get one as my first TL and I ALWAYS build stations in Yaki space. Why? Because there's plenty of room, you can actually build rep with them unlike other pirate factions so they won't blow your stuff up and as soon as I've got the first section of the hub plot done I can link Yaki space right into core systems.
