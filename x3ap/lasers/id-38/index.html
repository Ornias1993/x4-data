Prototype Matter/Anti-Matter Launcher

A weapon usually found equipped by corvettes or frigate-sized ships. The Matter/Anti-Matter Launcher fires a bomb-like projectile which contains a small amount of anti-matter suspended in an energy field. When the energy field is disrupted by impact or fails after a period of time, the anti-matter mixes with surrounding matter, resulting in a large explosion. This weapon only fires Matter/Anti-Matter Warheads.
