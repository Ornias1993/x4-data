The only missiles I use are Hurricanes (for M4 - M5 targets) and Hornets. The Hornets tend to get shot down though as they are pretty slow. But I can't live without my Hurricanes.

Missiles are one of those things you either love or hate. There is no real in-between. For a very long time I never used them but they are really good if used correctly and often. Makes a nice metagame experimenting with different ones to see what works best for your style. Things like Wasp I personally hate because they never hit it seems (they always just circle the target until they self-destruct) but others swear by them.
