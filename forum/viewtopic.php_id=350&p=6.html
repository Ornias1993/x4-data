Making use of Hephaestus Corp. I see. Be careful mixing SPPs and other stations in the same order. They have a habit of intersecting with each other, especially with Bliss Places.

As far as taking screenies goes, next time try tilting forwards and then hitting 8 on the numpad to swing the camera upwards to the target. You'll be able to get shots without having the ship hull in the frame that way.

I'll also point something out about sun. SPPs in 400% sun sectors don't give 4x the E-Cells as those in 100% sun sectors. Don't ask me why, because that would have been my guess also. In reality, they only give 50% more, and also require 50% more crystals. The resource/product ratio remains the same, so aside from the initial investment you're not actually going to benefit from high sun sectors. Also, when it comes to making energy, at least for use in player owned factories, it's more cargo efficient to make a crystal complex and export those to local SPPs than the cart E-Cells around.

Sorry if I'm being a buzz kill.
