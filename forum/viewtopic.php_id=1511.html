hi there, thanks for your questions. Your English is fine; ive seen a lot worse.

1) I have played not so a long time, but i noticed that trading is more hard with your mod.

Trading can not be directly effected. Its handled by the game, and will vary on every new game. You could argue trading might be harder, as there could be more enemies, although its hard to say; because each game has random things. So it will vary on every new start.

2) I remember a warning about installing ships in the Roguey's Mod. Is this why my ship is not working?

The reason behind this is that many of the ships use special models, textures, moving parts. To take them out of a mod requires a lot of experience, and even then they may not work. X3 isnt very forgiving, and will simply crash. Editing stats would be fine, but exporting/importing ships could cause some issues (ie. ids not aligning up). I dont know how cleaver the tools are, but usually a human is cleaver than a tool

When creating the mod, I made sure (as far as I could), that everything worked well together.

3) Traveling in new sectors, I noticed that they are apparently empty and with activity almost zero. Furthermore I do not see any activity about Xenon migration. Is this script, and the other scripts, not compatible with your mod? Should I wait for a while, before the sectors will become more active?

Again, this vary on game start. Things could happen in the future or not; I cant really say how every game will turn out. Also does every sector need tons of activity? isnt it more varied with sector with less happening? There are other sectors which are the opposite end, those being very busy. I wanted to make the universe and more varied place.
