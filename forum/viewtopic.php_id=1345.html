You can't get to OL until after the URV Forge is built.

Sparks


Two of the three Fusion Reactor producers in Albion are not PMC. Therefore, if you place Trade Agents on them and scan the info point for the Fusion Reactor-producing station module, you can check from anywhere in the galaxy how long it will be until they finish their next batch via the station into page. Then you can quickly switch over to the Trade interface and buy them with a trader like the plot Rahanas or another Rahanas Container (good for speed).

As long as the Defense Office on the trade ship is set to Defensive, PMC should not fire on it when it passes through Wrecksville (which has heavy PMC defenses near the Jump Beacon due to the Refuel station being close). To be on the safe side, it would be best to avoid being in-zone with the trader when it goes through Wrecksville. However, that is probably superstition on my part since PMC will probably not fire on your defensive civilian ships during the plot now.

AstroNutZ


I've been playing the game consistently (free play) since 3.0 came out, and here is some information that you may find useful. As you go up the tech tree, production facilities become more and more rare. There seems to be a bottleneck around certain items that bring the economy to a stand still. Things like fusion reactors are hard to get, but here is the best way to get the items you need:

1. Find a factory that has the item you need. Identify that production facility and look at the details of the (i) information icon or navigate the detail page to tell when the production facility will finish producing the next batch.

2. Wait till the batch is produced. I recommend talking to an NPC trader at the station to be a trade agent so you can go do other things while you wait. Maybe set an alarm on your phone to go off when the batch is produced.

3. As soon as the products are produced, buy them via trade computer. If you wait for more than a minute after the products are made... there already gone. This is because the economy is starving for certain products.

I recommend identifying where the bottleneck is and build those stations. Also, if you haven't been playing for 6+ hours, your economy is still in preset mode. It takes 6 or more hours before it works itself out. When starting a new game, I recommend leaving the game run over night while you dock at a station and allow the economy/trade system to develop.
