I have some old disks I keep for their authentity value, but I found that in <95% of the time you can copy it completely to a new disk or disk image and run it just fine, especially the older games. Might be worth trying, just be sure to keep the original.

I don't know the laws where you are but here in the Netherlands its perfectly legal to make a copy of copyrighted material for backup purposes as long as it is just 1 copy, is not distributed (personal use only) and you keep the original copy. This is because when you buy software (the game) you buy the right to use (play) it, regardless of what a EULA states. And since most companies will not replace a damaged copy...well, you get the idea. Not sure how it is in other countries though.
