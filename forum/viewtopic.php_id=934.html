They're isn't much difference in controls, only that the mouse will default steer your ship and you need to press a button to use it in menus, as compared to TC/AP which is the other way round. If you are used to navigating menus with the mouse, this can be a bit of a hit to your play-style, but if you used the keys you should feel right at home. There is a couple of menu changes, where none of it really shows on your cockpit, slight changes of design too, but is generally the same; there isn't any boarding, which feels weird now, but can make it a bit easier; combat feels a bit more balanced; there is an actual storyline and there is less ships. I would really recommend installing a mod like Roguey's X3 mod or XTM, as Reunion can't really stand up to the newer games as it simply has less features; adding one of those larger mods can fill the gaps and make it have more of an attraction over TC/AP; also there isn't much point staying Vanilla, as there is no achievements to miss out on, so I would strongly recommend going modified. I love reunion, probably my favourite X game, but it is starting to show its age to me...
