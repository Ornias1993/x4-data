Sorry I'm not familiar with the acronym CAG, but it might be like the other CPU commands. Sinxar sounds on the ball. Check the station's inventory. If it's full, the ship won't do anything. Or maybe you don't have money in the Hub's account. Otherwise check the station parameters. The CPU will scan for stations that offer the ware at the price you have selected, and standby until it detects one in the range you selected. If the economy is poor, there might not be wares on the stations. Also, you might have hostile stations that won't trade with your ships.
