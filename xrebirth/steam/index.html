How to add/update your Steam achievements
  • The first thing you need to do is have an account, if not already you can create one here,
  • Once you have a account, log-in, then link your account with Steam. This is done in Update profile,
  • When you have linked your account with Steam, come back to this page, refresh and click sync at the top right,
  • Please make sure your profile and game details are made public. This can be done in Steam's profile's privacy settings,
