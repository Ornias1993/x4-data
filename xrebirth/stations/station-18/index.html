Civ Ship Dockyard (station)

The Civilian Ship Dockyard produces vessels targeted at the mass consumer market. Driven more by economical considerations than technical necessities, operations of this sort also include extensive non-technical departments eg: market-research and marketing. End-customers can order their ships finished in a rich variety of fashionable colours, ranging from such as ocean blue up to desert orange.
