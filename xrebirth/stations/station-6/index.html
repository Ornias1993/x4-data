URV Parts Supply (station)

A URV Parts Supply complex (the abbreviation URV standing for unmanned ranging vehicles, or colloquially known as drones) is a highly-sophisticated technical manufacturing complex that incorporates many different lines of production, most of which are segregated from each other. Due to its complex nature, the URV Part Supply complex is run by a joint team of AGIs (artificial general intelligences) and engineers. Not unlike the Cell Fab Matrix, critical parts of this complex must be fully shut down before maintenance and servicing.
